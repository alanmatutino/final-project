What We Are About

What Clickkeys is, is a website meant for beginners and people new to the keyboard hobby. Here you can find links to different keyboard websites as well as information and websites on keyboard parts. We also include some sound tests of different switch types and please do note that the switch will vary depending on things such as mic quality, deskmat thickness, room temperature, room space, and other elements that affect the way something sounds.
